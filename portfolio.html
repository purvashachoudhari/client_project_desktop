Your Portfolio Hub

A professional portfolio is a powerful tool for showcasing your skills, projects, and growth. It gives potential employers a direct look at your work.

Portfolio FAQ

Do I need a portfolio for every job?

Not every job requires one, but it is highly recommended for design and technology roles.

Can I include group work?

Absolutely! Just be sure to clarify your specific contributions to the project.

Who to Contact for Portfolio Help

  • Email the UMSI 539 Teaching Team or visit the UMSI Career Development Office (CDO) for portfolio reviews, events, and appointments.
  • Find more portfolio advice and sample portfolios in the Application Materials section of the CDO Canvas Site.

Connect with the UMSI Career Development Office

The UMSI Career Development Office (CDO) offers personalized support through appointments, events, and resource guides. Visit the CDO website or find us on campus to learn more, schedule a coaching session, or just say hello.
